About Us

Green Lights Society

We are from Year-7 Fuchsia, Class-1, Group 2 from EDUSN Digital School.  We created this Smart Mobility website to raise awareness about eco-friendly transportation  and to help people understand how important it is to protect the environment.

Our Goal

Our goal is to promote smart mobility by encouraging people to use public transport, cycling, walking,  and other eco-friendly transport methods. We want to reduce pollution and make cities cleaner, safer,  and more sustainable for everyone.

Why It Matters

It matters because pollution and traffic are increasing every day. If we do not take action,  the environment will become more harmful for future generations. Smart mobility helps protect  health, reduce carbon emissions, and improve quality of life.

Our Idea

Our idea is to encourage a shift from private vehicles to eco-friendly transport systems.  We also want to raise awareness through education, better transport systems, and community action  so people can choose greener ways of travelling.
